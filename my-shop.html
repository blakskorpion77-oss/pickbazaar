How to connect Stripe
Stripe lets your customers pay by card. Takes 5 minutes to set up.
1
Go to stripe.com and create a free account
2
In Stripe: Developers → API Keys → copy your Publishable key
3
Paste it below and click Save
4
In Stripe: Payment Links → Create link → copy URL → paste when adding a product
5
Customers click "Buy Now" on your store, pay on Stripe, you fulfil via your supplier
