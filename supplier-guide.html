This guide walks you through exactly how to sign up with the world's best dropshipping suppliers, which ones work best for each niche, and how to connect everything to your PickBazaar shop so orders flow automatically.

✅Bottom line first: Start with CJ Dropshipping for USA orders (5–8 day delivery) and AliExpress/DSers for the widest product range. Add Spocket once you're selling to UK/Europe. That covers 90% of what you need.

How Dropshipping Actually Works

1

Customer buys from your shop

They pay via Stripe. Money goes directly into your Stripe account. You don't need to touch it yet.

2

You place the order with your supplier

Go to CJ Dropshipping / AliExpress / Zendrop and buy the same product, entering the customer's shipping address. You pay the supplier cost (e.g. $8).

3

Supplier ships directly to your customer

Your supplier packs and ships the product to your customer. You never touch or store the product. They ship it as if it's from you.

4

You keep the difference

Customer paid $29.99. You paid supplier $8. You keep $21.99 minus Stripe's 2.9% fee. That's your profit — no stock risk.

What You Need Before Connecting Suppliers

  • A Stripe account — stripe.com (free, takes 10 minutes). This is how you get paid by customers.
  • A business email address — Use Gmail to start (e.g. pickbazaarshop@gmail.com). Suppliers will email you.
  • A PayPal account (optional) — Some suppliers prefer PayPal. Sign up at paypal.com. Free.
  • Your bank details — You'll need these to receive payouts from Stripe and to pay suppliers.
  • A business name — "PickBazaar" or whatever you want. You don't need a registered company to start — many dropshippers start as a sole trader.
⚠️Most supplier accounts are free to create. Never pay for a "supplier list" or any upfront fee to access products. Legitimate dropshipping suppliers make money per order, not from signup fees.
⭐ Best Supplier for USA Selling

CJ Dropshipping

Why it's #1 for your shop: CJ has a warehouse in the USA. Products shipped from there arrive in 5–8 days — which is what American customers expect. They also have free API access, quality checks, and will do custom packaging with your brand on it.

1

Create your free account

Go to cjdropshipping.com → click "Sign Up" → use your business email. It's completely free.

2

Search for products

Use the search bar to find products in your niches — pets, auto, gym, fashion etc. Filter by "USA Warehouse" to ensure fast delivery to American customers.

3

Check the product listing

Look at: cost price, shipping time, stock availability, and reviews from other sellers. Request a sample ($3–10) before listing anything — always test quality first.

4

Add the product to your shop

In your PickBazaar Admin → Add Product. Note the supplier cost and set your selling price. Use the Profit Calculator to make sure you're earning at least 60% margin.

5

When a customer orders, fulfil via CJ

Log in to CJ → find the product → place order → paste in your customer's shipping name and address → pay CJ's cost price → they ship directly to your customer.

6

Get the tracking number

CJ sends you a tracking number. Email it to your customer. Update the order status in your Admin to "Shipped".

💡Pro tip: In CJ, go to My Products → Add to List for every product you sell regularly. This lets you fulfil orders in seconds rather than searching every time.
→ Visit CJ Dropshipping

AliExpress & DSers

AliExpress has 100M+ products — more than any other supplier. The downside for USA customers is shipping time (10–20 days standard). Use AliExpress to find products and test ideas, then switch to CJ Dropshipping for fulfilment once something sells.

1

Create AliExpress account

Go to aliexpress.com → Sign Up → use your email. Free. You'll use this to browse and research products.

2

Sign up for DSers (AliExpress official partner)

Go to dsers.com → Create account → connect to AliExpress. DSers is the official dropshipping tool — it lets you import products and process orders in bulk.

3

Visit the Dropshipping Centre

In AliExpress: go to aliexpress.com/dropshipping. This shows you products specifically available for dropshipping with sales data and supplier reliability scores.

4

Choose suppliers with ePacket or AliExpress Standard Shipping

These are the fastest options to the USA (10–15 days). Filter shipping method when searching. Avoid "China Post" — it can take 30–60 days.

5

When a customer orders

Log into AliExpress → find the product → place the order manually, entering the customer's shipping address. Or use DSers to bulk process multiple orders at once.

⚠️USA customers: Always be transparent about delivery times if using AliExpress. Say "10–15 business days" on your product pages. Underpromise and overdeliver. Switch to CJ's USA warehouse as soon as a product is selling well.
→ Visit DSers (Official AliExpress Partner)

Zendrop

Zendrop is perfect if you want automated fulfilment. Once connected, orders from your shop can be automatically forwarded to Zendrop who packs and ships without you doing anything manually. Strong US inventory with 3–7 day delivery.

1

Sign up at zendrop.com

Free plan available. Start free, upgrade to paid only when you're consistently making sales.

2

Browse the product catalogue

Search by category. Zendrop has strong Health, Beauty, Fitness, and Electronics categories. Look for "US Warehouse" badge for fastest delivery.

3

Import products to your list

Click "Import" on any product. Set your selling price. Zendrop shows you the cost price so you know your margin upfront.

4

Fulfil orders

When a customer orders, go to Zendrop → Orders → place the order with the customer's address. On paid plans this happens automatically.

→ Visit Zendrop

Spocket — UK & EU Suppliers

Spocket connects you with suppliers based in the UK, USA, and Europe. This means 2–5 day delivery to UK/EU customers. Great for Beauty, Fashion, and Home niches targeting European buyers.

1

Sign up at spocket.co

Free plan lets you browse products. Paid plan ($24/month) needed to import products and fulfil orders.

2

Filter by country

Use filters to show UK suppliers, EU suppliers, or US suppliers. Choose based on where your customers are to get the fastest delivery.

3

Import and fulfil

Add products to your shop with one click. When orders come in, fulfil through Spocket — they contact the supplier and handle shipping for you.

💡Spocket is the best choice once you start getting UK and European orders. Products arrive in 2–5 days instead of 2–3 weeks from China.
→ Visit Spocket

Printful — Print on Demand

Printful lets you sell custom branded products — t-shirts, hoodies, mugs, phone cases, tote bags — with your own logo. They print each item when ordered and ship from US facilities (3–5 days to USA).

This is the best way to build a real brand rather than just reselling generic products. Great for Fashion niche.

1

Create free account at printful.com

No monthly fee. You only pay per item when an order comes in.

2

Design your products

Use Printful's free design tool. Upload your logo, add text, pick colours. Start with t-shirts and tote bags — lowest risk, highest margin.

3

Order a sample first

Always order a sample of any product before listing it. Printful gives 20% off samples. Test the quality yourself.

4

Add to your shop and fulfil

When someone buys, manually order from Printful with the customer's address. On Shopify/WooCommerce this is automatic. For your PickBazaar shop, it's a quick manual step.

→ Visit Printful

Your Daily Order Flow

This is exactly what you do each day when orders come in:

1

Check Stripe for new payments

Log into stripe.com → Payments. Every payment is a new order. Note the customer name, email, and amount.

2

Email the customer to confirm

Send a quick email: "Hi [name], thanks for your order! We're processing it now and will send your tracking number within 48 hours." This prevents "where's my order?" messages.

3

Place the order with your supplier

Go to CJ/Zendrop/AliExpress. Find the product. Place the order. Enter the customer's shipping address. Pay with your card or PayPal.

4

Get tracking number from supplier

Your supplier emails you a tracking number (usually within 1–2 days). Forward it to your customer immediately.

5

Update your Admin

In PickBazaar Admin → Orders → log the order with customer name, product, sale price, supplier cost. Update status to "Shipped". Log the payment in Stripe & Payments section.

Time this takes: 15–30 minutes per day for up to 10 orders. At 20+ orders per day, consider AutoDS for automation.

Pricing for Profit

Use this as your minimum pricing rule:

Your Price = Supplier Cost × 3 (minimum)
Example: Product costs $8 from CJ → Sell for $24.99 minimum
This covers Stripe's 2.9% fee, occasional refunds, and leaves ~60% margin.
